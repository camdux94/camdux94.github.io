// About

Why I build.

I'm a fraud analyst at Stamps.com / ShipStation (Auctane) in Baldwin Hills, CA. Over six years I've worked identity verification, KYB/KYC, account takeover, and chargeback resolution — and I've watched a lot of that work stay manual longer than it needed to.

So I started building. BizVerify was the first — a business verification tool I shipped on my own that now has a GCP migration ticket with Okta auth, reviewed and greenlit by a Senior SWE II. That's not a side project anymore, that's infrastructure.

I'm targeting roles where fraud domain expertise and AI/automation skills sit in the same seat — AI Ops, Trust & Safety tooling, Fraud Engineering, or any team that wants someone who can identify the problem and build the solution.
